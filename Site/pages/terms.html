TERMS OF SERVICE

Billing Structure

  • Pricing and quotes are based off a rate of $60 (AUD) per hour of work.
  • Ad-hoc work will be billed at the above rate as well.
  • For set-price project work, once a price is agreed upon between Nomadic UX and the client, the client will be invoiced for a deposit amounting to 50% of the total price for the project. Upon completion, the final invoice for the remaining 50% will be sent to the client.

Payment

  • The client will be invoiced once the project is complete or on a specified date once a month if it is on-going. The invoice payment period starts from when the invoice is sent to the client.
  • Clients will have a 15 day payment period to pay the invoice in full. (exceptions can be made if discussed prior to payment being due.)

Late Fees

  • If payment is not received within the designated 15 day payment period, the client will incur a 5% late fee for every week that the invoice is overdue. Starting from the first day that the invoice becomes overdue (i.e. day 16 of a 15 day payment period.)
